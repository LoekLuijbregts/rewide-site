## 01 — Pastel Summer Sky
**Location:** Scheveningen, Den Haag  
**Format:** Portrait  
**Tags:** surf, sunset, pastel, Scheveningen, minimalist, woman, ocean, Netherlands

### Product page description
The North Sea at its most gentle.

This image was made at the moment between day and evening — when the sky decides which colour it will be and the answer turns out to be all of them at once. Blue into lavender into pink into gold, layered horizontally like a painting someone made with a very wide brush.

At the bottom of the frame: a single surfer, lying on her board, facing the horizon. Small against the sky. Unhurried.

Jop Hermans has photographed this coastline for years. He knows the light here better than most people know their own living rooms. This image is the result of that knowledge — being in exactly the right place at exactly the right moment, with the patience to wait for both to align.

*Particularly beautiful in the pastel palette of a bedroom or reading room.*

## 02 — Scheveningen Mood
**Location:** Scheveningen, Den Haag  
**Format:** Portrait — Black and white  
**Tags:** Scheveningen, pier, surf, black and white, storm, Netherlands, iconic

### Product page description
This is Den Haag on a real surf day.

Not the postcard version — the version that locals know. Heavy swell, grey sky, the pier emerging from spray in the background with the Ferris wheel turning despite everything. And in the foreground, dozens of surfers scattered across the water like punctuation marks, waiting, paddling, riding.

Jop Hermans made this image in black and white because colour would have been a distraction. The drama is in the tones — the dark water, the white of breaking waves, the silver of the sky, the silhouettes of people who chose to be out there anyway.

This is the Noordzee that surfers know. Not always beautiful in the conventional sense. Always honest.

*One of Jop's most-selected prints. Recommended for larger formats.*

## 03 — Soothing
**Location:** Scheveningen, Den Haag  
**Format:** Portrait — Black and white  
**Tags:** minimalist, black and white, surf, ocean, calm, two figures, Scheveningen

### Product page description
Two figures. An ocean. Everything else: negative space.

This is minimalism not as a stylistic choice but as an honest description of what was there. The North Sea on a quiet morning, the water pale and flat, two surfers wading out through the shallows. One ahead of the other. Neither looking back.

Jop Hermans has a gift for finding stillness in a subject — the sea — that is never actually still. This image captures that paradox: the water moves constantly in the frame, but the mood is one of complete calm.

It works in almost any interior. The restraint is the point.

*Sold as fine art print on Hahnemühle Photo Rag or Aluminium Dibond.*
