## 01 — The Atlas Crossing
**Location:** Morocco, Atlas Mountains, 2022  
**Published:** Rouleur Magazine (Landscape edition) · Bikepacking.com  
**Tags:** Morocco, Atlas, bikepacking, gravel, adventure, landscape, film

### Product page description
Ryan Le Garrec rode Morocco on a road bike with 25mm tyres.

Not because it was the sensible choice — it wasn't — but because it was the bike he had, and the road was there, and that combination has always been enough for him.

The Atlas crossing became a film. The film became an essay in Rouleur — the Landscape edition — and a featured story on Bikepacking.com. The photographs that came back from the trip are what you would expect from someone who learned to shoot under the influence of his father, a war reporter who believed the honest image was always more important than the clean one.

Ryan's Morocco work is blurred in places. Underexposed in places. Framed in ways that a photography school would flag as errors. That is exactly what makes them work. They feel like memory rather than documentation — the way a place lives in you after you have ridden through it alone.

## 02 — Tour Divide — Great Basin
**Location:** Wyoming/Nevada, USA, 2019  
**Context:** Filming Tour Divide race with Josh Ibbett (Hunt Bike Wheels)  
**Tags:** Tour Divide, USA, landscape, ultra-cycling, documentary, vast

### Product page description
The Great Basin. Late afternoon. The sky black where it met the mountains and gold where it met the horizon.

Ryan Le Garrec was here to film a rider named Chris Seistrup, who was third in the Tour Divide and riding through one of the emptiest landscapes on the American continent with a huge smile on his face. Chris stopped when he saw the camera. He handed Ryan a sticker. He said: *I think I'm third in the race now. Please share a shot with me.*

Ryan has his sticker on his bike now.

The photograph from that afternoon captures something the film could not: the scale of what these riders move through alone. A figure in an enormous landscape. The loneliness that is also freedom.
